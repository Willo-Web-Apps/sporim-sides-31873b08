# Design DNA — SIDES

## Visual Personality: dark-modern
## Layout: product-led
## Typography: bold-display

### Color Palette
- Primary (electric green): #00C853 → oklch(0.72 0.22 145)
- Accent (fired-up orange): #FF5722 → oklch(0.64 0.22 32)
- Surface (deep near-black): #0B0F0E → oklch(0.09 0.006 145)
- Surface mid: oklch(0.12 0.008 145)
- Surface card: oklch(0.15 0.01 145)
- Text primary: oklch(0.96 0.005 145)
- Text muted: oklch(0.55 0.01 145)

### Font Pairing
- Display: Archivo Black — 900 (bold, athletic, unapologetic headlines)
- Body: Barlow — 400/500/600 (clean, sporty, mobile-readable)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Scoreboard-at-night hero**: Deep near-black with asymmetric green glow from top-left and orange ember from bottom-right — feels like stadium floodlights, not a tech product
2. **Telegram bot mockup UI**: A phone-style chat bubble mockup in the hero showing the bot in action — makes the product tangible for Telegram-native users without screenshots
3. **VS divider**: The How It Works section uses a bold centered "VS" typographic element to reinforce the challenge/duel DNA of the brand — unusual section treatment that no competitor uses
4. **Noise grain overlay on dark surfaces**: Adds texture that prevents the flat-dark "AI slop" look — subtle feTurbulence at 3% opacity keeps it premium
5. **Sports icons as glowing stat pills**: Upcoming matches shown as glowing dark cards with team flags/emoji and live odds feel — scoreboard aesthetic applied to content
